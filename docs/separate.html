🎛️ The Magic of Stem Separation

Turn one song into four separate tracks!

🎵 What's Stem Separation?

Imagine you baked a cake and now you want to separate the flour, sugar, eggs, and chocolate chips. That's basically what stem separation does—it takes a finished song and magically pulls apart the vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments into individual tracks!

🍕 Think of it like this:

You order a supreme pizza with everything on it. Stem separation is like having a machine that separates it back into: just the cheese, just the pepperoni, just the vegetables, and just the crust. Now you can eat (or mix!) each part separately!

1

Select Your Audio

Go to your project and find the audio file you want to separate in the Upload tab. Click on it to select it— it'll highlight to show it's ready. It's like tapping a friend on the shoulder to say "Hey, you're the chosen one!" 👆

2

Switch to Separate Tab

Click the Separate tab at the top of your project view. This is where the magic happens! You'll see the selected audio file displayed prominently, like a celebrity on a magazine cover. 📰

💡 Quick Access: After you select an audio file in the Upload tab, there's a "Separate" button that appears right next to it. Click that and boom—you're already in the right place!
3

Choose Your Separator

At the top of the Separate tab, you'll see two options:

🤖 Demucs

The popular AI-powered separator. Great for high-quality results on full songs.

Default

🎵 Spleeter

The speedy alternative! Fast separation but needs a beefy computer.

Beta
4

Pick Your Model (Demucs Only)

If you chose Demucs, now pick your fighter! Each model is like a different chef:

HT Demucs

The reliable all-rounder. Good results, reasonable speed. Like a trusted home cook.

Recommended

HT Demucs FT

The "Fine-Tuned" version. Slower but sometimes delivers better quality. Like a Michelin-star chef—worth the wait!

MDX

A lighter model. Faster but might miss some details. Quick & decent!

MDX Extra

The heavy hitter of MDX family. More detail, more time.

5

Choose Output Mode

How many stems do you want? Think of this as choosing pizza size:

4️⃣

4-Stem

Vocals + Drums + Bass + Other
Full remix control!

2️⃣

2-Stem (Vocals)

Vocals vs Everything Else
Perfect for karaoke!

⚠️ Model & Mode Combo Alert!

Not all models play nice with all modes. If you pick 4-Stem, only HT Demucs and HT Demucs FT will work reliably. Other models will be grayed out— it's not you, it's them! 😅

6

Press the Magic Button!

Ready? Click either:

  • 🎛️ "Separate into 4 Stems" for the full experience
  • 🎤 "Extract Vocals + Accompaniment" for karaoke mode

And then... wait. Patience, young padawan! 🍵 The AI is working hard to analyze and separate your audio. You can watch the progress right there.

🤓 Did you know? The technology behind stem separation uses deep neural networks that have "learned" what different instruments sound like by listening to millions of songs. It's like teaching a robot to recognize pizza toppings by showing it 10 million pizzas!
🎯 Pro Tips:
  • Longer songs = longer processing — A 3-minute song takes about 5-10 minutes
  • Check Admin Jobs if you want to see all the gory details of your processing
  • Don't refresh the page — The job keeps running even if you look away!
🎉

What Happens Next?

Once separation completes (success! 🎊), you'll find shiny new stem files in your asset list. They're automatically sent to the Mixer tab where you can start blending them together!

Next stop: The Mixer! Head over to the Mixer tab to adjust volumes, pan positions, and create your perfect mix. It's like being a conductor, but you control everything!
