1

Create Your First Project

Projects are like folders—they keep your audio files organized. Click the "Projects" button in the top navigation, then hit the big "+ New Project" button. Give it a cool name like "My Awesome Mix" or "Experiment #42" (we won't judge). 🎯

2

Upload Your Audio

Now comes the fun part! Open your new project and go to the "Upload" tab. You can drag and drop your audio files (MP3, WAV, FLAC—we're flexible!) or click to browse. It's like feeding your pet, but instead of a cat, it's digital audio files. 🐱🎵

📁 Supported Formats: MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, OGG, and more! If it's audio, we've got you covered. We've even got your back if it's NOT audio—we'll probably just stare at it confused. 🤔
3

Explore Your Assets

Once uploaded, your files appear in the asset list. You can:

  • Preview them by clicking the ▶️ play button
  • Rename them by clicking on the name (we won't tell anyone)
  • Delete them if they misbehave (they can't cry, so it's okay) 😢
  • Get fancy with BPM and Key detection (more on this later!)
